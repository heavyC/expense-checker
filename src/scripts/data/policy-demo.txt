Travel & Expense Policy Document

- Meals: max $75/person for client meals, $25 for solo meals 
- Travel: economy class required for flights under 4 hours 
- Hotels: max $250/night in major cities, $175 elsewhere 
- Receipts required for all expenses over $25 
- Alcohol not reimbursable unless client entertainment 
- Expenses must be submitted within 30 days
